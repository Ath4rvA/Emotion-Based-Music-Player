  • Basics

  • How do I use the application?

    The first step is to sign up with the application.
    In the second step use the credentials generated to login.
    A menu will appear on successful login.

  • How do I sign up?

    On the main page use the slider functionality to go right, there is an option to sign up. Just fill in the necessary information and press the sign up button.
    You are ready to go!

  • How do I login?

    On account creation, it should redirect you to the login page. If not then head to home page and you will see a button which says Log in. On pressing the button it will ask for your credentials. Enter valid credentials and you will have successfully logged in.

  • Is account registration required?

    Yes, account registration is required so as to help us monitor the system. Don't worry! We don't ask for much of your information. And the email and password are kept secured in our database.

  • How do I contact the developers?

    On the main page you can see a button on the tool bar which says contact us. Press the button and you'll be able to see the necessary information you need to contact us!

  • Is the application free to use?

    Yes! The application is completely free to use.
