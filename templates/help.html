Help

Following are some of the steps regarding how to use the system :

  1. If you are new user then, first sign up using email, password, confirm password field.
  2. Once you are registered user, simply logged in using your authentic email and password
  3. After successfully logged in,you will have a search bar page in front of you,like google,enter text information regarding a video query you want to search.
  4. After clicking search button,you will directed to a page where you will get the list of ten videos ranked by our system.
  5. On the next page after clicking a ShowYouTubeList button,you will directed to a page where you will get a list of videos which YouTube displays on their page for similar query.
